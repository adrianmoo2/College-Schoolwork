Donor Relations and Stewardship

Home /  Courses And Programs / Donor Relations and Stewardship
    • SHARE:
    • Twitter
    • Facebook
    • Google Plus
    • LinkedIn
    • Email a Friend

Explore how you can improve the quality of your relationship with your donors and increase support for your organization. Learn to discern the interests of individuals in your donor base. Develop appropriate fundraising strategies and plans for your donors including: "packaging" development needs to match donor interests, soliciting the donation, ways of involving potential donors before and after donations, providing ongoing recognition and opportunities for giving. Learn why many organizations have implemented “donor stewardship” activities that bring donors closer to the outcomes they make possible, confirming that the organization is fulfilling its responsibility to utilize funding as the donor intended.

Course Number: BUSA-40930
Credit: 2.00 unit(s)
Related Certificate Programs: Fundraising and Development

There are no sections of this course currently scheduled. Please contact the Business, Professional & Legal Programs department at 858-534-8131 or unexbusa@ucsd.edu for information about when this course will be offered again.
